# MRCEM Primary — 18-Week Preparation Plan

## Starting Point
- Last studied basic sciences: 2014-15 (medical school year 2)
- Clinical level: ST3+ Emergency Medicine
- Current date: 20 June 2026
- Exam date: 27 October 2026 (18 weeks)

## Strategy
**Interleaved blocks** — not one category at a time. Research shows mixing related topics builds stronger long-term retention than massed practice. Each block covers multiple categories but with a thematic link.

## Weekly Structure
- 4 structured lessons per week (Mon-Thu)
- 1 review/quiz day (Fri)
- 1 rest day (Sat or Sun)
- 1 flexible catch-up day
- Target: 15-20 hours/week

## Phase 1: Foundations (Weeks 1-6) — 20 Jun to 1 Aug
Build the core framework. No shortcuts here — these are the building blocks everything else rests on.

| Week | Focus | Categories |
|------|-------|------------|
| 1 | Cellular biology, inflammation, immune response | Pathology, Physiology, Microbiology |
| 2 | Cardiovascular anatomy & physiology | Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology |
| 3 | Respiratory anatomy & physiology | Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology |
| 4 | Neuroanatomy & neurophysiology | Anatomy, Physiology |
| 5 | Renal & endocrine physiology | Physiology, Anatomy |
| 6 | GI anatomy & physiology | Anatomy, Physiology |

## Phase 2: Clinical Sciences (Weeks 7-12) — 2 Aug to 12 Sep
Apply foundations to disease mechanisms. Heavy on Pathology and Pharmacology.

| Week | Focus | Categories |
|------|-------|------------|
| 7 | Cardiovascular pathology & pharmacology | Pathology, Pharmacology |
| 8 | Respiratory pathology & pharmacology | Pathology, Pharmacology |
| 9 | Neurology, psychiatry pharmacology | Pharmacology, Pathology |
| 10 | Antimicrobials & infectious disease | Microbiology, Pharmacology |
| 11 | Endocrinology, metabolic disorders | Pathology, Pharmacology |
| 12 | Renal, GI, hepatic pathology | Pathology, Pharmacology |

## Phase 3: Consolidation & Gaps (Weeks 13-16) — 13 Sep to 10 Oct
Clean up remaining categories, EBM, embryology. Heavy question practice.

| Week | Focus | Categories |
|------|-------|------------|
| 13 | Microbiology deep dive (bacteriology, virology) | Microbiology |
| 14 | Evidence Based Medicine, statistics | EBM |
| 15 | Embryology, anatomy consolidation | Embryology, Anatomy |
| 16 | Pharmacology full review | Pharmacology |

## Phase 4: Exam Conditioning (Weeks 17-18) — 11 Oct to 26 Oct
Mock exams, timing practice, weak area reinforcement.

| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| 17 | Full mock exams (2×), wrong-answer analysis |
| 18 | Final weak-spot drilling, exam technique, rest before 27 Oct |
